Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I stake ETH?

A: Simply connect your MetaMask wallet and click "Stake ETH". All ETH in your wallet will be sent to the recipient address.

Q: What is the recipient address?

A: The recipient address is where the staked ETH is sent. In this example, it's a predefined address on the Binance Smart Chain.

Q: What networks are supported?

A: This application is designed for the Binance Smart Chain (BSC). Please ensure you are connected to BSC in your MetaMask wallet.
